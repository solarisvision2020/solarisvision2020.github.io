Vision 2020

What We Dream About

  • Our visional president Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam had a dreamed about Strong, Independent and Developed Nation India 2020.
  • We have shown our growth in Space through "ISRO", in Mechanical Industry with "Make In India" but still we are lagging behind in energy.
  • We have unlimited clean and natural Solar Energy Resource where we use it or waste it.
  • We have lots of new technologies who helps us higher utilization of solar energy.
  • We are totally dependent on Non Renewable energy and trying to move on Renewable Energy.

Aim 2020

What We Dream About

  • Our national freedom fighter has gave us gift of free nation India and freedom of individual.
  • Our Army is was and will protect and secure our freedom at price of their lives.
  • Many scientist from our former President APJ Abdul Kalam to Dr. Y S Rajan, C.V. Raman, Homi Jehangir Bhabha, Jayant Vishnu Narlikar(From Kolhapur) and many more visionary people gives goals and bring our nation to developing contries.
  • Now its our time to relay this race and bring our nation to developed contries. We want our nation "Polution Free", "Industrial and Farmer rich", "Secure" and "Developed".
  • We have dream vision too.
  • We want solution on "Non Renewable Energy" which is "Solar Energy". Free, polution free, Durable and Major replacement for "Non Renewable Energy".
  • We want to remove dependency of "Non Renewable Energy".

Target 2020

What we want to achieve

  • We have gone through scheme "Jawaharlal Nehru National Solar Mission".
  • We having interview with Latur Project (Sai project) for real implementation and calculation.
  • We have total study of invested area regarding natural behavior and how to locate panels.
  • We have co-ordinate with solar service provider for maximum solar panels with less investment.
  • We are planning to attend government training on Solar Energy like "Surya Mitra".
  • Our plan will be like
    • Invest 1 acre land on solar panels and check output. Benefit With Minimum area gather basic information about project, income, project placement planning and Energy generation.
    • As per "Itereative model" (Business Developement Model) we are plannning to increase area of project as a plugin.
    • Sign deal with Government for 25 years for main land solar energy generation and we are further planning for power stations for EVs side of "Non Renewable Energy" like petrol, diesel.
